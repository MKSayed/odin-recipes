Indomi

Description

Noodles is a tasty japanese dish.

Indomi was mainly invented to help the poor people who doesn't have enough time or money to be able to eat something that makes then feel full and able to do their daily work with the minimum amount of money and time required.

Ingredients

  • Boiling water
  • Seasoning (Comes inside the Indomi's package)
  • the noodles it self

Setps of making a delicious Indomi

  1. Boil some water
  2. Put the Indomi into the water for 2 minutes
  3. add the seasoning to the inodmi (As preferred)
  4. Serve the Indomi into a dish and voilla!
